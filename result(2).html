what is mobile app development?

Mobile app development is the process of creating software applications that run on mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. This involves designing, building, testing, and deploying mobile apps for various operating systems, such as iOS and Android. Mobile app developers use programming languages and development tools to create apps that meet the specific needs and requirements of users. The goal of mobile app development is to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience for mobile users while achieving the desired functionalities and features of the app
